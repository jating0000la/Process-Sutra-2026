## Introduction

ProcessSutra ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our FlowSense workflow management platform (the "Service"). 

By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect:
- **Contact Information:** Email address, first name, last name
- **Profile Information:** Profile picture, job title, department
- **Organization Information:** Company name, domain, logo, billing information
- **Authentication Data:** Login credentials, authentication tokens

### 2. Workflow and Content Data
- **Process Data:** Workflows, forms, and business processes you create
- **Collaboration Data:** Comments, shared workflows, team interactions
- **File Uploads:** Documents, images, and other files you upload
- **Usage Patterns:** How you interact with workflows and features

### 3. Technical Information
- **Device Information:** IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Analytics:** Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Performance Data:** Load times, error logs, system performance metrics
- **Session Data:** Login times, session duration, activity logs

### 4. Communications
- **Support Communications:** Messages, emails, and support tickets
- **Platform Notifications:** System messages and alerts
- **Marketing Communications:** Newsletters and promotional materials (with consent)

## How We Use Your Information

### 1. Service Provision
- Provide and maintain the FlowSense platform
- Process and store your workflows and business data
- Enable real-time collaboration features
- Provide customer support and technical assistance

### 2. Platform Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
- Develop new features and functionality
- Optimize platform performance and reliability
- Conduct research and analytics for service enhancement

### 3. Communication
- Send system notifications and service updates
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Provide security alerts and important notices
- Send marketing communications (with your consent)

### 4. Security and Compliance
- Monitor for security threats and unauthorized access
- Investigate and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
- Maintain audit trails for compliance purposes

### 1. Retention Periods

- **Deleted Accounts:** Personal data deleted within 30 days
- **Workflow Data:** Retained for 3 years or as per organization policy
- **Audit Logs:** Retained for 7 years for compliance

### 2. Extended Retention
Data may be retained longer when:

### 3. Physical Safeguards
