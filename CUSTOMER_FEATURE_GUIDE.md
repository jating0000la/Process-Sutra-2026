## ğŸ“ **Glossary (Terms Explained)**

**Flow/Workflow:** A series of connected tasks that complete a business process

**TAT (Turnaround Time):** The deadline for completing a task

**Task:** A single step in a workflow assigned to someone

**Form:** Digital form to collect information during workflow

**Simulator:** Tool to test workflows before going live

**Bottleneck:** A slow step in the workflow that delays everything

**System:** A collection of related workflows (e.g., "Order Processing System")

**Rule:** Instructions that determine what happens next in a workflow

**Status:** Current state of a task (pending/completed/overdue)

**Doer:** Person assigned to complete a task
