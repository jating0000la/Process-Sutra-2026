d) Is rightfully received by Recipient from a third party without breach of any confidentiality obligation;

## 2. OBLIGATIONS OF RECIPIENT

### 2.1 Non-Disclosure

Recipient agrees to:

a) Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence;

b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without Provider's prior written consent;

c) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose (as defined in Section 2.3);

d) Protect Confidential Information using the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, but in no event less than reasonable care;

e) Limit access to Confidential Information to employees, contractors, and advisors who:
   - Have a legitimate need to know;
   - Have been informed of the confidential nature of the information;
   - Are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as those in this Agreement;

f) Maintain a written record of all persons to whom Confidential Information is disclosed.

### 2.2 Security Measures

Recipient shall implement and maintain appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect Confidential Information, including but not limited to:

a) Access controls and authentication mechanisms;
b) Encryption of data in transit and at rest;
c) Regular security audits and assessments;
d) Employee training on confidentiality obligations;
e) Physical security measures for premises and equipment;
f) Network security and firewall protection;
g) Incident response procedures;
h) Regular backup and disaster recovery procedures.

### 2.3 Permitted Use ("Purpose")

Recipient may use Confidential Information solely for:

a) Evaluating the Platform for potential use;
b) Using the Platform in accordance with the Terms of Service;
c) Integrating the Platform with Recipient's systems and processes;
d) Training Recipient's employees on Platform usage;
e) Complying with applicable laws and regulations;
f) Any other purpose explicitly authorized in writing by Provider.

### 2.4 Prohibited Uses

Recipient shall NOT:

a) Reverse engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software or technology;
b) Use Confidential Information to develop competing products or services;
c) Copy or reproduce Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose;
d) Remove or alter any proprietary notices or labels;
e) Transmit Confidential Information outside of approved geographic regions without consent;
f) Use Confidential Information for any unlawful purpose;
g) Sublicense, sell, or transfer Confidential Information to third parties.

- The fees paid by Recipient to Provider in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim; or

a) Breaches of confidentiality obligations;
